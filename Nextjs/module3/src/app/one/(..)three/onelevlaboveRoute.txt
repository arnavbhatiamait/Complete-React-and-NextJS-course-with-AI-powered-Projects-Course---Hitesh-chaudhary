One Level Above Route
In Next.js, a One Level Above Route is created using the (..) convention inside the app
router.
It allows you to show another route one level above the current layout without fully navigating away.
This is commonly used for:
Nested layouts
Breadcrumbs
Contextual navigation
app/
│
├── dashboard/
│   ├── page.js
│   └── @settings/
│       └── (..)profile/
│           └── page.js
