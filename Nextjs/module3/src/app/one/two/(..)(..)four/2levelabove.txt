2 level above Route
In Next.js, a Two Level Above Route is created using the (..)(..) convention inside the
app router.
It allows you to show another route two levels above the current layout without fully navigating away.

app/
│
├── dashboard/
│   ├── analytics/
│   │   ├── page.jsx
│   │   └── @modal/
│   │       └── (..)(..)settings/
│   │           └── page.jsx
│
├── settings/
│   └── page.jsx


Other Interception Patterns
Pattern	Meaning
(.)about	same level
(..)photo	one level up
(..)(..)settings	two levels up
(...)login	from app root
