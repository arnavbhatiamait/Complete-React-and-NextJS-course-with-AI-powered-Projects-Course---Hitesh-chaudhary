3 level Intercepted Route
In Next.js, a Three Level Intercepted Route is created using the (...) convention inside the app router.
It allows you to show another route from the app root without fully navigating away.
This is commonly used for:
Global modals
Universal overlays


app/
│
├── shop/
│   ├── products/
│   │   ├── details/
│   │   │   ├── page.jsx
│   │   │   └── @modal/
│   │   │       └── (..)(..)(..)cart/
│   │   │           └── page.jsx
│
├── cart/
│   └── page.jsx
