Same Level Intercepted Route
In Next.js, a Same Level Intercepted Route is created using the (.) convention inside the app router.

It allows you to show another route inside the current layout without fully navigating away.

This is commonly used for:

Modals
Side panels
Overlays
Preview pages


app/
│
├── feed/
│   ├── page.js
│   └── @modal/
│       └── (.)photo/
│           └── [id]/
│               └── page.js
│
├── photo/
│   └── [id]/
│       └── page.js
